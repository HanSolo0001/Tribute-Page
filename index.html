Legos

A brief history of legos up to present day.

The Lego logo that we know today was created in 1972.

The Lego time line:

  1. 1932 - The Lego Group began in the workshop of Ole Kirk Christiansen making wooden toys.
  2. 1934 - The Lego Group becomes known as Lego, which means "play well".
  3. 1947 - Lego begins making plastic toys.
  4. 1949 - Two years after starting the production of plastic toys, Lego creates the early version of the Lego brick based on the Kiddicraft Self-Locking Bricks.
  5. 1936 - Lego's motto "only the best is good enough" is created.
  6. 1958 - the modern brick design was developed and patented; it took five years to find the right material for it, ABS (acrylonitrile butadiene styrene) polymer.
  7. 1969 - Lego Duplo bricks are created for younger aged children.
  8. 1978 - Lego minifigures are created.
  9. 2013 - A 1:1 scale of an X-Wing was put on display in New York City. It was the largest's display up to that time, and it consisted of over five million bricks!
  10. 2015 - Lego replaced Ferrari as the "world's most powerful brand.

"With a bucket of Lego, you can tell any story. You can build an airplane or a dragon or a pirate ship - it's whatever you can imagine."
